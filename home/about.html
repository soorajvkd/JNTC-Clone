About Us

Jamia Nadawiyya Training College owes her genesis and efflorescence.

Our History

Jamia Nadawiyya Training College owes her genesis and efflorescence to KNM (2/1957), the most progressive muslim religious organization founded in 1950. The college was established in 1991 and maintained by the central committee of its organization. Jamia Nadwiyya trust board was constituted for the purpose.

Our Vision

To evolve into a premier teacher education institution , molding eminent teachers with creative minds , innovative ideas and sound practical skill and to shape a future where human values are included for the enrichment of mankind.

Our Mission

To evolve into a premier teacher education institution , molding eminent teachers with creative minds , innovative ideas and sound practical skill and to shape a future where human values are included for the enrichment of mankind.

Our Objectives

01

We wish to make the college a great storehouse of learning , a great bureau of standards, a great workshop of knowledge, a great laboratory for the training as well of men of thought as of men of action. The college would serve as an instrument for the conservation of knowledge, for the discovery of knowledge, for the distribution of knowledge, and above all, for the creation of knowledge-makers.

02

The institution has the vision to educate the students to assume social obligations-to train as excellent teachers to solve social problems to help set ethical directions for society. The college is concerned with student growth and development, the discovery and refinement of knowledge, and to increase the social impact on the community.

03

The college shall pay attention to enable the students to be: “caring for oneself ,including one’s health ,caring one’s family ,friends and peers; caring for other people ;caring for students and co-workers, caring for the social ,economic and ecological welfare of one’s society and nation ;caring for human rights; caring for other spices; caring for the livability of the earth; caring for truth, knowledge and learning” .
