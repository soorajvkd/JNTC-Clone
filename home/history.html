About Us

Jamia Nadawiyya Training College owes her genesis and efflorescence.

Jamia Nadawiyya Training College owes her genesis and efflorescence to KNM (2/1957), the most progressive muslim religious organization founded in 1950. The college was established in 1991 and maintained by the central committee of its organization. Jamia Nadwiyya trust board was constituted for the purpose. Dr. Usman Sahib, President of K.N.M was the first chairman of the trust board which established the college.

The college aims to build an enlightened, strong and prosperous nation through education. The responsibility to mould the destiny of the students depends to a large extent on their teachers who take pain to nurture and develop them. Jamia Nadwiyya Training College has premium faculty and those teacher educators have contributed significantly to inspire and inculcate patriotic feeling among the student teachers. Under their guidance, J.N.T.C has always remained as the torch bearer of the nation's most cherished noble ideals and reflects on the nation's main ethos, aspirations and concerns. It is one of the premier teacher education institutions in the state of Kerala offering secondary education courses and aims to create ideal teachers who help their students to blossom their dreams and to create a developed nation.

Jamia Nadwiyya training college provides top quality education to pre-service student teachers with well established infrastructure, human resource and curricular programmes. As the students come out from the portals of this institution, they develop their body, mind and personality. Capacity building for decision making, inculcating values such as respect for civic and social intuitions and getting sound knowledge in pedagogical practices are the key areas promoted in this college. Transmission and transformation of culture all get manifested in various activities under-taken through curricular, co-curricular and extra curricular activities of the college. Democratic practices are promoted through community living camps and individual development is attained through self study, supervised study and computer assisted instruction.

It strives to produce school teachers with caliber, competence and commitment. After all, we offer courses with the unique imagination. We try to familiarize the pre service student teachers with the changing profile of the profession and get them familiarized with the latest techniques and technology. Learning to know learning to do, learning to live together and learning to be, are the four pillars of the educational programme. The college strives to liberate the trainees from ignorance and ill health. It aims to create an enlightened society, modern in performance scientific in out look and yet committed in traditional values. The attitudinal changes will be effected through technology enabled class rooms.

It caters to the needs of the students of rural and backward area and serve all people irrespective of caste, creed and religious affiliation. This teacher education institution is growing from strength to strength, giving emphasis to positive education that may lead to positive thinking and wish to build a prosperous and well developed nation in the immediate future.

Molding next generation teachers as professionally competent and socially responsible citizens are the noble aims of Jamia Nadwiyya Training College (J.N.T.C). Since its inception, the college has had created a niche for itself, and is recognized as an innovative teacher education centre treading a different path. J.N.T.C is considered as a hall mark to teacher education, and it is evident from the ever growing demand for its students. With the mushrooming of institutions offering teacher education, it has become the responsibility of Jamia Nadwiyya to take lead and offer quality education uncompromisingly.
