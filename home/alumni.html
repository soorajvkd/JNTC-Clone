About Alumni

Jamia Nadawiyya Training College established in 1995 with an intake of 100 students and at present we are very effectively running the institution with the same intake and vigour . Almost 2800 students already undergone BEd course from this institution. They are now serving in many fields including schools . colleges , Universities , etc in India and abroad. We started our journey with the student Beena Bai Moorkath as our first registered student for BEd in the batch 1995-96 and like her , the other students who got BEd from here are very proudly proclaiming that we are nurtured from this prestigious institution . Hence it is very worthy to mention here such persons as our prominent ALUMNI members. ALUMNI of Jamia Nadawiiya Training College is formed and actively undertaking various programmes and activities in and out side the campus .
